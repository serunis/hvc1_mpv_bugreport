The color of HVC1 footage becomes abnormal with a specific nvdec option. --profile=default
the sample footage is taken by Samsung galaxy s24+ with HEVC encodnig (HVC1).

It plays well with
ffplay -vcodec hevc_cuvid 20240710_121243.mp4
mpv --hwdec=nvdec ./20240710_121243.mp4 --profile=high-quality
mpv --hwdec=nvdec ./20240710_121243.mp4 --profile=fast

However it is bad with
mpv --hwdec=nvdec ./20240710_121243.mp4 --profile=default
